0 The Harris's Sparrow, Zonotrichia querula, is a large sparrow.
0 Their breeding habitat is the north part of central Canada .
0 In fact, this bird is Canada's only endemic breeder.
0 In the winter they migrate to the Great Plains states of the United States, from lower South Dakota to upper Texas.
0 The common name of this species commemorates the American amateur ornithologist Edward Harris .
